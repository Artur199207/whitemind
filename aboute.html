About Us

Our Mission

 We support individuals on their journey of self-discovery and personal growth through training programs that are based at the intersection of Psychology and business, as well as on the combination of theory and practice in the fields of Psychology and Experience Management.

Our Vision

To create a space where everyone can develop inner harmony and skills for successful communication, strengthening relationships in both personal and professional life.

Our Origin

At WhiteMind, we believe that every individual has the potential for personal growth and development. Our story began with a simple yet powerful vision — to create a space where people can openly explore their thoughts and feelings while acquiring the skills necessary to enhance their quality of life and interactions with others.

WhiteMind is a center founded by a group of professionals in Psychology and Experience Managemen, born out of a desire to help people not only understand their emotions but also learn to manage them. We strive to create an atmosphere where everyone feels accepted and supported, regardless of their background or experience. Empathy is at our core, allowing us to deeply understand and support our clients on their journey toward personal growth and self-awareness.

We assist individuals in recognizing and managing their emotions in a supportive environment. Additionally, we offer business trainings aimed at developing Emotional Intelligence level, improving Customer Experience and enhancing personal effectiveness.
Our training programs are based on scientifically grounded methods and best practices, enabling us to effectively combine theory and practice. We take pride in helping our participants not only achieve their goals but also find inner harmony.

Why Whitemind?

Whitemind is a center created by experts in Psychology and Experience Management. Our goal is to help you "untangle" complex emotional knots and establish associative connections, making the learning process more effective and meaningful. We create a safe space for openly exploring your experiences, which fosters deep self-discovery and support.

Whitemind is not about emptiness and complete tranquility. It is about honesty with oneself, accepting one's inner world with all its emotions, and striving for peace. We assist you in making sense of your thoughts, understanding the reasons behind your feelings, and thereby achieving true clarity and inner balance.

We believe it is important not just to focus on what you feel here and now but also to understand why those feelings have arisen. Our programs help convey and recognize your emotions through associative images, linking them to everyday things, which allows for a deeper understanding and experience of your inner feelings.

Whitemind is a training center offering programs for personal and professional development aimed at unlocking potential and achieving harmony.
